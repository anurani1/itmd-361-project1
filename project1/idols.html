Some Stage Choreographies I Like

One of my interests is idols; I enjoy a lot of the music produced by idol groups, as well as the effort that goes into the performances. One of the aspects of a good performance is the choreography. Here are some I like!

Antifragile - Le Sserafim

This choreography is very smooth!! All of Le Sserafim's tracks have such insane advanced choreography. They are amazing performers to watch.

In my opinion, anyway.

Kitsch - IVE

Again, I really like smooth choreography! Plus, the artists look like they're genuinely enjoying themselves and their performance. Bonus points for cool jackets.

Pirate - Everglow

Not only is the song addicting, there is a very addicting dance to go along with it. Women are so cool.

These opinions are my own and the reader is under no obligation to agree. However, I think everyone should agree that women are pretty cool.
